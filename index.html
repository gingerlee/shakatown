All classes are intended to be welcoming, creative, and inspiring — a little hard, a little easy, relaxing and hopefully v enjoyable. ☮️

I offer yoga outside as community service, rooted in social justice, aiming to bring people together and inward, reflecting our best selves. I teach trauma-informed yoga with an open heart and open mind to help us all stay healthy, connected, and present.

I also really just love meeting new friends, being in nature, being active. And being new to Sisters — what better way to do this than with the freedom of yoga outside? 🍍
