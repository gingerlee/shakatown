Cliff Clemens Park 🏔 Sisters, Oregon

Join me for an hour long vinyasa flow yoga class. Yoga and so much more — dynamic movements synced to breath, add some sneaky squats + core to boot! Simply, a good chill workout. 🤸‍♀️

Wanna come?
